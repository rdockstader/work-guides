Support Phone Guidelines

Guidelines for answering phone calls

Curtis Cutis | Sept 19, 2018

When Answering Phone Calls

Every effort should be made to help avoid confusion for callers who are not intending or expecting to hit the support line. Calls from the front desk should be answered a bit differently than those coming from the Support extensions.

When a call comes from one of the support extensions, we should be answering it with something like:

  • "support, this is <Your Name>" or
  • "support, how may I help you" or
  • "support, this is <Your Name>, how may I help you"

When Calls come from the front desk (typically sown on yoru phone as "operator" or "Donna" we should be answering it with:

  • "Journal Technologies, how may I help you" or
  • "Journal Technologies this is <Your Name>, how may I help you"

Never give out the extension of our CEO (Jerry Salzman) or Jon Peek

  • If Jon Peek is not available, transfer to his voicemail.
  • If a call comes in for JErry, contact Maryjoe Rodriguez.
  • If Maryjoe is not available, transfer to her voicemail.
